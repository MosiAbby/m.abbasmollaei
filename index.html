About Me

I received my B.Sc. degree in Computer Engineering from Shahid Beheshti University, Tehran, Iran, in 2015. Then, I started my master's studies at University of Tehran in Computer Engineering (Hardware Architecture) and I received my M.Sc. degree in 2018. I'm currently a Ph.D. student at University of Montréal, Polytechtique Montréal, Montréal, Canada.

My research interests are Programmable Networking, Digital Design, Embedded Systems, and Configurable Systems.
